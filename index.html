Hi, I'm Alex!

I'm a 19 year old Math and Computer Science Major from Wayland, MA, currently living in Pittsburgh, PA at Carnegie Mellon University. I really enjoy coding and problem solving, and have done a couple hackathons and made a couple projects, including this website. My first programming language I learned was Java, so that is the one that I'm most familiar with and my favorite one to program in, but I've learned some other ones along the way, including Python and the HTML, CSS, JS set (still learning the libraries of JS though) through classes, projects, and self-study. I'm also very interested in biology, and I have published a first-author research paper in the journal "Purinergic Signaling" and several co-authored research papers.

Some fun facts about me: I'm a musician; I've played the piano for 16 years and the violin for 11 years. At some point, I will probably include some of my playing on the website. Also, since I'm from Boston, I'm a pretty big Celtics fan, and as of the time I am writing this, their season is going well, so shout out to them.
